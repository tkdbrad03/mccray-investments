Financing & Payments

No bank. No credit check. Period. McCray Investments finances the land directly — we are the bank. Your payment history with us is what matters, not your credit score.

Owner financing means you make payments directly to McCray Investments instead of a bank. Here's how it works:

  • You put down a down payment to secure the lot
  • You make monthly payments over your chosen term (24, 36, or 48 months)
  • Once all payments are complete, the deed transfers to you and you own the land free and clear
You are building equity from day one. The land is yours as long as you keep your payments current.

Most properties require a $2,500 down payment to get started (the Lake City bundle requires $5,000 down). The interest rate on all owner-financed properties is 7% per year.

You choose your term — 24, 36, or 48 months — and your monthly payment is fixed for the life of the contract.

Monthly payments depend on which property you choose and which term you select. Here's a quick overview:

  • $14,900 properties (Walnut St, Lake City lots) — from $297/mo (48 mo) to $557/mo (24 mo)
  • $19,900 properties (304 S Fifth St) — from $413/mo (48 mo) to $775/mo (24 mo)
  • $21,000 properties (301 S First St) — from $443/mo (48 mo) to $831/mo (24 mo)
  • $74,900 bundle (Lake City 6 lots) — from $1,646/mo (48 mo) to $3,090/mo (24 mo)

All payments are at 7% interest. View exact payment options on each property's detail page.

Absolutely. Cash buyers get a clear title quickly and there are no interest charges. Cash pricing is listed on every property page. Contact Tasha to get the process started — cash deals close fast.

Payments are due on the 1st of each month. There is a 10-day grace period — meaning if your payment is received by the 10th, no late fee is charged.

Tip: Set up a recurring payment or reminder so you never miss. Communication goes a long way — if you're ever in a tough spot, reach out to Tasha before your payment is late.

Here is our full missed payment policy:

  • Days 1–10: Grace period — no fee, no action
  • After day 10: A $35 late fee is added to your balance
  • After 2 consecutive missed payments: The account enters default and a formal default notice is sent
  • 30-day cure period: You have 30 days from the default notice to bring your account current (pay all missed payments + late fees)
  • If not cured: The contract is terminated and the property reverts to McCray Investments. All payments made are forfeited per the land contract agreement
Life happens. If you're struggling, contact Tasha immediately. We'd rather work something out than go through default.

Yes — there is no prepayment penalty. You can pay off your balance at any time and receive your deed immediately upon final payment. Many buyers choose to pay extra each month to pay off faster and save on interest.

Ownership & Title

The deed transfers to you once your final payment is made and your balance is paid in full. At that point you own the land outright — free and clear with no liens.

During the payment period, you have possession and use of the land under a land contract / contract for deed agreement.

Yes — all properties are offered with clear title. Land is acquired through the tax lien sale process which clears prior liens as part of the acquisition. If you have specific concerns, we recommend a title search through a local SC title company — typically $150–$300.

Property taxes in Florence County and Lake City are relatively low on vacant residential land. The county land value assessments for our properties and taxes are calculated based on assessed value.

Once you close and own the property, annual property taxes are your responsibility. We recommend checking with the Florence County or Lake City tax assessor for the current tax amount on any specific parcel.
